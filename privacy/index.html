Privacy Policy

PropSize · Last updated: 9 April 2026

PropSize is a position size calculator for prop traders. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable law including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR.

1. Who we are

PropSize is operated by Justice Ughelie, based in Ireland. You can contact us at hello@propsize.app.

2. What data we collect

PropSize is designed to work with minimal data collection.

Data stored locally on your device only: Your firm selection, account size, risk preferences, instrument settings, and contract size overrides are stored using AsyncStorage on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Subscription data (RevenueCat): When you purchase a subscription or start a free trial, RevenueCat processes your payment through Apple. RevenueCat stores an anonymous subscriber identifier linked to your Apple ID to manage your subscription status. We do not have access to your payment card details. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Push notifications: If you grant permission, we may send you push notifications about your trial status. Your device token is used by Expo to deliver notifications and is not shared with third parties.

Exchange rate data: On app open, PropSize fetches current exchange rates from frankfurter.app to ensure accurate calculations. No personal data is sent in this request.

3. What data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, trading history, or any personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics tools, crash reporting services, or advertising networks. We do not track your behaviour within the app.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

For subscription management, we process data on the basis of contract performance — specifically, to provide you with the subscription service you have purchased. For push notifications, we process data on the basis of your explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time in your device settings.

5. Data retention

Local device data is retained until you delete the app or clear app data. Subscription data held by RevenueCat is subject to their retention policies. Push notification tokens are retained until you withdraw permission or delete the app.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to processing and to data portability where applicable. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@propsize.app. As we hold minimal personal data, most requests can be fulfilled by deleting the app from your device.

7. International transfers

RevenueCat is a US-based service. Data transferred to RevenueCat is subject to standard contractual clauses or other appropriate safeguards under GDPR.

8. Children

PropSize is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at propsize.app/privacy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@propsize.app.
